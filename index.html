About Me

I like to consider myself a bit of a nomad. I was born right outside of Atlanta, Georgia in November of 1990. At a very young age I moved to Florida, where I resided until I was twenty-four years old. I decided to return to my roots in Georgia for a couple of years, before moving out to Denver, Colorado, where I remained until early 2021. I have since ended up in the great Chicagoland area.

I have many hobbies, most of which are outdoors including: camping, hiking, fishing, hunting, boating, rock climbing, and many others.

If there is good food involved, definitely count me in. Whether its a backyard BBQ or trying out a new restaurant (hole-in-the-wall's welcomed), I'm there.

I also have a strong passion for the visual arts, most specifically photography and cinematography.

I'm a huge sports fan. My favorite sport is football (or as many would call soccer), but I am also a true fanatic and admirer of martial arts.
